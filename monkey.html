DUSKY LEAF MONKEYS

The dusky leaf monkey, spectacled langur, or spectacled leaf monkey (Trachypithecus obscurus) is a species of primate in the family Cercopithecidae. It is found in Malaysia, Burma, and Thailand.

Dusky leaf monkeys are medium-sized primates with a body length of 50 to 70 cm (20 to 28 in) and a tail length of 60–80 cm (24–31 in). They have a distinctive coat of grayish-brown fur, with a lighter underside and a characteristic white ring around their eyes. Their diet consists mainly of leaves, fruits, and flowers.
